About This Match Score

How Your Score is Calculated: This compatibility score combines visual preference analysis (50%), psychometric assessment (35%), genetic compatibility (10%), and a serendipity factor (5%). The visual component uses transfer learning to predict attraction patterns. Psychometric alignment is based on the Cardinal Driver framework. Genetic scores reflect HLA complementarity research.

Limitations: This score is a probabilistic estimate, not a guarantee. Human attraction is complex and multi-dimensional. Our model has been trained on diverse datasets but may not capture all cultural contexts or individual preferences.
